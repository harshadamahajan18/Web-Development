SEO(Search Engine Optimization)

  1. Keyword Research and Optimization: Identify relevant keywords and incorporate them naturally into your content, titles, and meta descriptions.
  2. Quality Content: Create valuable, informative, and engaging content that meets user intent and encourages longer site visits.
  3. Title Tags: Craft unique, descriptive, and keyword-rich titles for each page, adhering to a character limit (typically under 60 characters).
  4. Meta Descriptions: Write compelling meta descriptions that summarize your content and include target keywords, staying within 150-160 characters.
  5. Heading Tags: Use heading tags (H1, H2, H3, etc.) to structure your content and include keywords to improve readability and SEO.
  6. Alt Attributes for Images: Add descriptive alt attributes to images, including relevant keywords to help search engines understand the image content.
  7. SEO-friendly URLs: Create clean, descriptive URLs that include primary keywords and use hyphens to separate words.
  8. Internal Linking: Link to other relevant pages within your website to enhance navigation and help search engines crawl your site.
  9. Mobile Optimization: Ensure your website is mobile-friendly with responsive design, as mobile usability is a significant ranking factor.
  10. Page Speed: Optimize your website's loading speed by compressing images, leveraging browser caching, and minimizing CSS and JavaScript to improve user experience and search rankings.

Web Vitals

  1. Largest Contentful Paint (LCP):
    • Measures: Loading performance.
    • Goal: LCP should occur within 2.5 seconds of when the page starts loading.
  2. First Input Delay (FID):
    • Measures: Interactivity.
    • Goal: FID should be less than 100 milliseconds.
  3. Cumulative Layout Shift (CLS)::
    • Measures: Visual stability.
    • Goal: CLS should be less than 0.1.
